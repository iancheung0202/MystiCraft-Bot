Q: I'm having an issue with my account or the game.
A: Please provide detailed description of your issue with screenshots or videos. Include step-by-step process to reproduce it to help us identify the issue quicker.
---
Q: My items disappeared due to lag or despawn, can I get them back?
A: If items were lost due to lag, despawn, or auto-clear systems, we do not refund or restore those items. 
---
Q: I need a refund for my purchase.
A: In your Billing Support ticket, provide your transaction ID or the email associated with your purchase. Include details about why you need a refund and we'll review your case.
---
Q: I died in combat because of lag or disconnect, can I get items restored?
A: If you disconnected or lagged out during combat and died, we do not return items or undo combat deaths. This includes deaths caused by mobs or players while frozen/disconnected.
---
Q: Can I appeal for someone else?
A: No. You can only appeal for your own Minecraft account. We do not process appeals for Minecraft accounts that are not created by the account’s owner.
---
Q: I want to appeal for my ban in the Discord server.
A: We generally do not process appeals for punishments in the Discord server (we only do so for punishments done in the Minecraft server) unless you have extenuating circumstances.
---

A: You may have reached the ticket cooldown limit. Different ticket types have different cooldowns (e.g., Appeals: 14 days, High Tier Testing: 30 days). Try again later or create a different ticket type.

Q: How do I switch from cracked to premium account?
A: Log in with your cracked account, run `/premium <yourpassword>`, log out, then log back in with your premium account. Your premium account must have the exact same username as your cracked account, and the cracked account will no longer be used after migration.

Q: I want to report a bug in the Minecraft server.
A: Please make sure you have included a detailed description of the bug and steps to reproduce it. You must provide clear video proof of the bug occurring.
---
Q: I want to report a player who was cheating, hacking or misbehaving.
A: Make sure you have detailed their IGN, exactly what they did wrong, and video proof. Screenshots alone are not sufficient. Video evidence is required to prevent false reports.
---
Q: I want to report a staff member for unfairly punishing me.
A: If you are appealing for your punishment in-game, please create an Appeal ticket instead. Staff Report tickets are reserved for reporting their other behaviours, such as racism, NSFW, or hacking.
---
Q: I lost items due to a server-side glitch/bug, can I get them back?
A: Item restoration due to bugs is handled case-by-case. You must provide clear video proof of the bug occurring. Without proof, we cannot restore items.
---
Q: Can I transfer purchases or ranks between accounts?
A: Purchases, ranks, and perks are non-transferable between accounts.
---
Q: I encountered a hacker but couldn’t record in time or don't have video evidence.
A: Without video proof we cannot take action against any players. Please try to screen record future encounters.
---
Q: Can staff speed up my request if it's urgent?
A: All tickets are handled in order and as soon as our staff members are available. Urgent requests are not guaranteed priority unless deemed critical by staff.
---
Q: Can I submit additional evidence after creating a ticket?
A: You are more than welcome to send additional messages in your existing ticket with more proof or clarification.
